## Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Deepgram Speech-to-Text cost per hour?

Pay-As-You-Go pricing for Nova-3 (our standard model) is **$0.29/hour** for monolingual streaming and **$0.35/hour** for multilingual. Flux, our premium conversational model purpose-built for voice agents, runs **$0.39/hour** monolingual and **$0.47/hour** multilingual. Growth plan rates are about 12.5%

Are older models (Nova-2, Enhanced, Base) still available?

Yes. These remain available at unchanged rates for existing deployments: Nova-2 streaming at **$0.35/hour**, Enhanced at **$0.99/hour**, and Base at **$0.87/hour**. Growth plan rates are about 12.5% lower. We recommend new projects start with Nova-3 for general-purpose transcription or Flux for real-time voice agents. Both are surfaced in the main rate tables above.

Does Deepgram charge for silence or round up audio time?

No. Deepgram uses **true per-second billing**. If your audio file is 14 seconds long, you pay for exactly 14 seconds. Many competitors round up to the nearest 15 seconds or full minute, which can inflate your actual invoice by 15-20%.

What is included in the $200 free credit?

Every new account receives **$200 in free credit**, which is equivalent to approximately **43,000 minutes (over 700 hours)** of transcription using our Nova model. Unlike "free tiers" that expire after 12 months, this credit is available until you use it up, allowing you to prototype without time pressure.

How do you calculate costs for multichannel audio?

We bill based on the **total duration of processed audio**. If you process a 10-minute file with 2 channels (stereo), you are billed for 20 minutes of processing. This ensures you only pay for the exact amount of speech data analyzed.

What is the difference between Pay-As-You-Go and Growth plans?

**Pay-As-You-Go** requires no upfront commitment and bills monthly based on usage. The **Growth** plan requires a commitment (starting at $4k/year) but unlocks up to a **~20% off** across our products, higher concurrency limits, and priority support.

Are there extra fees for real-time streaming vs. pre-recorded audio?

No. Uniquely in the industry, Deepgram charges the **same low rate** for both real-time streaming and pre-recorded (batch) transcription. We do not charge a premium for the low-latency infrastructure required for streaming.

How does pricing work for the Voice Agent API?

The Voice Agent API is billed based on the **duration of the conversation**. It includes the orchestration of Speech-to-Text (listening), LLM processing (thinking), and Text-to-Speech (speaking). If you "Bring Your Own" LLM (e.g., GPT-4), you only pay Deepgram for the audio components and orchestration, often resulting in lower costs.

Do Audio Intelligence features cost extra?

Yes. Features like **Summarization, Topic Detection, and Sentiment Analysis** are billed per input unit (per hour or per 1,000 tokens) in addition to the transcription cost. However, because these run on our specialized small language models, they are highly cost-effective compared to running raw text through a generic LLM.

Is Text-to-Speech (Aura) billed by the minute or character?

Deepgram Aura TTS is billed by **input character** (e.g., $0.015 per 1,000 characters). This allows for precise cost control, as you are only charged for the text you actually convert to speech, regardless of how slowly or quickly the voice speaks.

Can I deploy Deepgram on-premise or in a private cloud?

Yes. For Enterprise customers with strict data sovereignty or security requirements (such as banking or healthcare), we offer **self-hosted containers** that you can deploy in your own VPC or on-premise hardware (requires NVIDIA GPUs).

What happens if I exceed my concurrency limit?

On the Pay-As-You-Go plan, requests exceeding your concurrency limit (e.g., 100 concurrent streams) may be queued or rejected. To avoid this, you can upgrade to a **Growth** or **Enterprise** plan, which offers custom concurrency limits to match your peak traffic needs.

Is Deepgram HIPAA and SOC 2 compliant?

Yes. Deepgram is **SOC 2 Type 2 certified** and **HIPAA compliant**. We can sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for Enterprise customers handling sensitive healthcare data.

Do you offer volume discounts for high-usage applications?

Yes. If your application processes millions of minutes per month, our **Enterprise** plan offers significant volume discounts, custom model training, and dedicated account management.

## Managing your Deepgram Pay-As-You-Go credits: auto-load settings, refunds, and transfers

At Deepgram, we offer a flexible **pay-as-you-go** service where customers can purchase credits for usage on our platform. Our goal is to provide transparency and flexibility, while ensuring that you have full control over your account and payments.

Auto-Load Functionality

For your convenience, we provide an **auto-load function** that automatically recharges your account when your credit balance falls below a certain threshold. By default, your account is set to **reload with $100 when remaining credit is $10**.

- You can turn off auto-reload at any time through your account settings.
- It is the customer’s responsibility to manage their auto-load settings to avoid unwanted charges.

Managing Your Subscription

We encourage all customers to review their credit balance and auto-load settings regularly to ensure they align with your needs. By purchasing credits and using our services, you acknowledge that you are fully responsible for the integrity of your uploaded files and coding systems.

Refunds for Unused Credits

We offer refunds for **unused credits** that were purchased within the **last 30 days**.

- Refunds will only apply to credits that have not been used.

Credits purchased more than **30 days ago** are **non-refundable**, as they represent services that were available to you at the time of purchase.

Refund Request Process

To request a refund for unused credits purchased within the last 30 days, please complete this [form](https://forms.gle/PryPGvQTZm98Agso7). We will review your account and process any eligible refunds promptly. If you have any other questions please contact ConsoleBilling@deepgram.com.

Disclaimer for Incorrect Audio Files

Please note that Deepgram is not responsible for errors in your uploaded files due to issues in your recording or transcription systems, including but not limited to **loops in the audio** caused by coding or system errors on your end.

- If an incorrect or faulty file is uploaded, Deepgram’s system will process the file as received. Customers are still responsible for paying for all processed files, regardless of any errors resulting from their own system or coding.
- We strongly recommend reviewing your files and system before uploading them to avoid such issues. Deepgram provides the service of processing the audio files that are submitted and is not liable for customer errors.

Credit Transfer Policy

Credits purchased from Deepgram are tied to the specific account under which they were originally acquired.

- **Purchased Credits** may be transferred between accounts **within the same organization** upon request and subject to verification and approval.
- **Promotional or Free Credits** are **non-transferable** under all circumstances, including between accounts owned by the same organization.

We reserve the right to decline transfer requests that appear to be an attempt to circumvent our credit usage policies or promotional terms.

If additional credits are needed in a specific account, we recommend purchasing credits directly through that account’s billing profile.
